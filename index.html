Meet Our Doctors

Village Green Chiropractic is a family practice dedicated to serving the Marietta community. We are proud to be led by a father-son team who share a passion for holistic health and patient care.

Dr. Joseph Sennebogen Sr., DC ("Dr. Joe")

With years of experience and a gentle touch, Dr. Joe has helped countless patients find relief from pain and achieve better health through natural chiropractic care.

Dr. Joseph Sennebogen Jr., DC ("Dr. Joey")

Following in his father's footsteps, Dr. Joey brings fresh energy and a deep commitment to wellness, ensuring that every patient feels heard and cared for.
