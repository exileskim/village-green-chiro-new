Our Philosophy

A Holistic Approach to Care

Sennebogen Chiropractic is a family operated office that provides patients with a holistic approach to care. Our goal is to help you achieve a more effective life, to restore and maintain full function, and ensure your overall well-being.

We focus on the human body and how it functions by using a variety of chiropractic and soft tissue techniques. These techniques address dysfunction in the bones, joints, and muscles of the spine and extremities.

Meet Us

Our Doctors

Founder & Chiropractor

Dr. Joseph Sennebogen Sr., DC, CCSP

Dr. Joseph Sennebogen Sr. attended Life University in Marietta, Georgia, where he graduated with a doctorate degree in Chiropractic in 1987.

He is a board Certified Chiropractic Sports Practitioner and also received a Certified Personnel Trainer Degree from The National Academy of Sports Medicine.

Dr. Joe has been providing chiropractic care to families and local athletes in the Marietta area for more than 36 years.

Chiropractor

Dr. Joseph Sennebogen Jr., DC, MS, CCSP

Dr. Joey received his Bachelor's Degree in Biology from Georgia College in Milledgeville, Georgia. He graduated Summa Cum Laude with his Doctorate Of Chiropractic from Life University and with his Master's Degree in Sports Health Science.

He is a Certified Chiropractic Sports Practitioner (CCSP). While finishing his Master's Degree, Dr. Joey worked under Dr. Keith Rau as a team chiropractor for Life & Kennesaw State University.

Dr. Joey is certified through Titleist Performance Institute (TPI), which allows a unique approach to golf swing biomechanics and injury prevention.
